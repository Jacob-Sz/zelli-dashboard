338 ad-click recordings, watched click-by-click. Here’s where the money leaks.

Every US Facebook/Instagram session from Jun 16–29 was opened in the Microsoft Clarity player and its full click-event timeline was read — no sampling, nothing skipped. Each recording below carries a note describing exactly what that visitor did.

Audit window: Jun 16–29, 2026 (all 338 recordings) · Companion quote DB: now 1,356 real quotes / $302K quoted pipeline through Jul 6, 2026 (2 booked, 0.15%) · refreshed 2026-07-07 19:30

The funnel, as it actually happened

Three leaks, ranked by money

Blocked buyers — form never gave a price

39 of 198 engaged (20%)

They clicked 6–30 times and typed their info, but the form never responded with a quote. 41 sessions logged dead clicks — the FB/IG in-app browser intermittently ignores taps. Two users were blocked on two separate visits.

Fix: test /lp/auto inside the real Facebook & Instagram in-app browsers; find why taps don’t register; add a plain-link fallback on the CTA. Recovers ~20% of engaged traffic.

Saw the price, backed out

33 of 111 quote-reachers (30%)

They completed the flow, viewed the quote, then navigated back to the landing page and left. Price shock, missing trust signals, or no obvious next step — either way, they already know the number.

Fix: capture a phone number before the price so these can be texted; add reviews/guarantee + one big next-step CTA on the quote page.

/call-now/tx ad page is dead on arrival

~100% bounce from Google/YouTube ads

Sessions arriving from google.com, syndicatedsearch.goog and youtube.com land on /call-now/tx and leave in 1–7 seconds with zero clicks. A whole paid channel buying a page nobody acts on.

Fix: verify click-to-call from a real ad click; otherwise repoint the campaign at the working quote flow or pause it.

  1. Fix the /lp/auto form in the FB/IG in-app browser.Dead-click evidence in 41 sessions; 39 proven buyers lost. This is the single highest-value fix.
  2. Capture a phone number before showing the price.Turns the 33 price-rejectors (and every expired quote) into a follow-up list one text away from closing.
  3. Strengthen the quote page.Trust signals + one clear next-step CTA where 30% of quote-reachers currently turn around.
  4. Fix or pause the /call-now/tx campaign.Google/YouTube spend currently lands on a page that converts nobody.
